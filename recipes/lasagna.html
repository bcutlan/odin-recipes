Lasagna

Description

Layers of tender pasta stacked with rich meat sauce, creamy béchamel, and melted cheese. Hearty, comforting, and baked until bubbling and golden.

Ingredients:

  • Lasagna sheets (pasta)
  • Ground beef
  • Pork (optional)
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Olive oil
  • Tomato passata or crushed tomatoes
  • Tomato paste
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Dried oregano or basil
  • Bay leaf
  • Butter
  • Flour
  • Milk
  • Nutmeg
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese

Steps:

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. Heat olive oil in a pan and sauté onion and garlic until soft.
  3. Add ground beef (and pork if using) and cook until browned.
  4. Stir in tomato paste, passata, herbs, salt, and pepper; simmer 20–30 minutes.
  5. Prepare the béchamel by melting butter, whisking in flour, then milk and nutmeg.
  6. Cook lasagna sheets according to package instructions (if required).
  7. Spread a thin layer of sauce in a baking dish.
  8. Layer pasta sheets, meat sauce, béchamel, and cheese; repeat layers.
  9. Finish with béchamel and a generous topping of Parmesan.
  10. Bake for 35–45 minutes until golden and bubbling; rest before serving.
