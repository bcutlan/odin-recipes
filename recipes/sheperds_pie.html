Shepherds Pie

Description

A hearty British classic made with savory minced lamb in rich gravy. Topped with creamy mashed potatoes and baked until golden.

Ingredients:

  • Ground lamb
  • Onion
  • Carrots
  • Frozen peas
  • Garlic
  • Olive oil
  • Tomato paste
  • Worcestershire sauce
  • Beef or lamb stock
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Fresh thyme or rosemary
  • Potatoes
  • Butter
  • Milk

Steps:

  1. Preheat the oven to 200°C (400°F).
  2. Peel and boil the potatoes until tender.
  3. Mash potatoes with butter, milk, salt, and pepper; set aside.
  4. Heat olive oil in a pan and sauté onion, carrots, and garlic.
  5. Add ground lamb and cook until browned.
  6. Stir in tomato paste, Worcestershire sauce, and herbs.
  7. Pour in stock and simmer until thickened.
  8. Add peas and cook for a few more minutes.
  9. Transfer meat mixture to a baking dish and spread mash on top.
  10. Bake for 25–30 minutes until golden and bubbling.
