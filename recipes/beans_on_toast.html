Beans on Toast

Description

A classic comfort dish of warm, saucy baked beans served over crispy toast. Simple, filling, and perfect for a quick meal any time of day.

Ingredients:

  • Baked beans (canned)
  • Bread slices
  • Butter
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Cheddar cheese (optional)
  • Worcestershire sauce (optional)

Steps:

  1. Open the can of baked beans.
  2. Pour the beans into a small saucepan.
  3. Heat the beans gently over medium heat, stirring occasionally.
  4. Add a splash of Worcestershire sauce if desired.
  5. Season the beans with a little salt and pepper.
  6. Toast the bread slices until golden and crisp.
  7. Butter the toast while still warm.
  8. Spoon the hot beans evenly over the toast.
  9. Sprinkle grated cheddar cheese on top if using.
  10. Serve immediately while hot.
